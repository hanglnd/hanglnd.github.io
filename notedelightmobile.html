NoteDelight Mobile App


This is the mobile version of the NoteDelight project. Due to the limited space on mobile screen, the challenges lie much in prioritizing which features and elements to display in the screen and which to hide away. I did quite an intensive research on other popular mobile apps such as Flipboard, Facebook, Pinterest... to see how they effectively design their content and navigation elements.

Launch Screen

Home Screen

I kept the header simple and clear by only including 2 main tabs (Home and Notebooks), and 2 important control icons of adding a new note and search function. The rest of settings are stored in the "more" icon. Each notebook section is also kept simple by only displaying the recent notes in card format where user can apply actions upon them by a long press (color, favourite, delete or share). Under each section, users can quickly add a new note into the notebook by hitting the add button. This design is highly usable as notes and notebooks are presented as card objects with easily identifiable features like colors or covers to help users quickly scan through and locate the item they need.
